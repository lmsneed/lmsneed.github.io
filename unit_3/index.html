Creative design for your small business. My love story with graphic design and beautiful imaging began a long time ago. It's been a passion but also a big part of my regular day job for the last 10 years. The idea of having my own business was always somewhere in my mind, but the timing was never right.

The last years were full of surprises and projects, which transformed my life in a beautiful chaos. It's within this chaos that, in 2015, Dumont Design Graphique was finally brought to life.

My office, that was for a long time made of an anonymous working space with grey carpet and fluorescent lighting, is now made of a vintage table with view on the lake and mountains, and the sound of kids playing and laughing.

Creating professional high quality designs at affordable prices is what Dumont Design Graphique is about. You work hard on your business, and you can rest assured that I will work as hard day after day to create unique designs that will make your products or services stand out.

A good design isn't only a beautiful one. It also has to be the reflection of who you are and what your business is about. To achieve this ideal combination of aesthetic/efficiency in my designs, not only do I set my standards really high, but I also make communication with my clients an important aspect of every project.

Do not hesitate to contact me if you have any questions, I'm always happy to ear from you.
